Music

Listening to music is my favorite past time. My music tastes come from a wide variety of genres and languages. I analyze personal changes in music taste and reflect on how those changes have come about.

Anime

One of the ways I like to enjoy myself and expand my creative abilities is through the viewing of anime. I watch shows from various years, and appreciate those that go above and beyond to show creative merit. In the anime club I am able to discuss positive and negative aspects of certain animes with like minded individuals. This has helped me build my analytical skills of visual media.

Programming

After taking EECS 101 at the University of Michigan, I became interested in computer science as a minor. Since then I have taken various classes on the subject, and will soon be complete with the requirements for a minor. My programming interests lie in website and mobile development.

Amateur Photography

After borrowing a Nikon D100 from my father, I became interested in using amateur photography as a way to relieve stress. I purchased a Nikon D3300 and have been learning the ins and outs of amateur photography. Some pictures I have taken are available on Flickr.

Board Games

While many find board games simple fun, I find them a way to build critical thinking and strategy skills. For example, games like King of Tokyo teach how to assess risk through a simple dice mechanic. Others, such as Sushi Go!, build decision making skills through simple probability problems.
